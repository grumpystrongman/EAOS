## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is EAOS?
Enterprise AI agent orchestration with policy, approvals, and auditability.

### Is EAOS vendor-neutral?
Yes. EAOS supports multiple model providers and self-hosted models.

### How is leakage prevented?
Default-deny controls, policy gates, approval gates, and immutable audit trails.
